No cloud. No account. Just your phone.

Mäuse runs entirely on your device. Your expenses never leave your phone — there's no server, no database, no sign-up. It's just code.

  • Local-first storage
    All expenses live in IndexedDB on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.
  • Works offline
    Manual entry works without any internet connection. Open the app and go.
  • No account, no tracking
    No sign-up, no analytics, no telemetry. Zero data collection.
  • Portable backups
    Export your data as JSON. Import it on another device. You own the file.
  • One optional cloud connection
    Voice mode sends audio to OpenAI for transcription using your own API key. Everything else is 100% offline. The key stays on your device and is excluded from backups.
